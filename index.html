The Journey

Born in Sijhora, Madhya Pradesh, Venkat Raman Singh Shyam is a contemporary torchbearer of the Gond art tradition. As the nephew and apprentice to the legendary Jangarh Singh Shyam, Venkat has evolved the 'Jangarh Kalam' style, blending ancient tribal motifs with modern narratives.

His work captures everything from the quiet rhythm of village life to the chaotic energy of global events. He has exhibited globally, from the National Gallery of Canada to the Queensland Art Gallery in Australia.
